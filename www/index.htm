Welcome to Enhanced

Enhanced Electronics and Communication Services, Inc., is a leading provider of Telecommunication Products, Services and Networking Solutions in the Philippines, delivering end-to-end networking and communication infrastructure and services to Telco Companies, Banking Institutions, Enterprise Manufacturers, and the Power and Electronics Sector.

For more than 20 years, Enhanced has been deploying and maintaining the most mission critical of systems for the biggest corporate names in the country.
